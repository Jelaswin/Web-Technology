Metro Project Phase II Nears Completion

Officials have announced that the second phase of the Coimbatore Metro project is on track for its scheduled completion. This new line will connect the airport to the city center, significantly reducing travel time for thousands of commuters daily. Local businesses are optimistic about the economic boost this will provide.

VOC Park Unveils New Botanical Garden

The iconic VOC Park and Zoo has opened its newly renovated botanical garden to the public. The garden features a diverse collection of native and exotic plants, a new greenhouse for tropical species, and interactive educational displays. The initiative aims to promote biodiversity and environmental awareness among residents.
